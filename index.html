Track your path to financial independence.

onFIRE is a personal net-worth and FIRE-progress tracker. Connect your accounts, see your full financial picture in one place, and know exactly how close you are to financial independence.

What it does

Unified net worth
Aggregates balances across checking, savings, brokerage, retirement, and loan accounts.
Investment tracking
Follows holdings and performance across taxable and tax-advantaged accounts.
FIRE progress
Calculates your FI number, savings rate, and projected timeline to independence.
Secure by design
Bank connections use Plaid. Credentials are never stored or seen by onFIRE.

How account connections work

onFIRE uses Plaid to securely connect to supported financial institutions. When you link an account, you authenticate directly with your bank — onFIRE never sees or stores your banking credentials.
